Visibility Distance

Visibility Distance

Visbility distance or sight distance is a measurement of the distance of an object or light can be clearly seen. It also depends on the transparency of the surrounding air, as such it is unchanged no matter the ambient light level or time of day.

Below is the visualization:

Normal visibility is where the distance of an object or light are transparent and clearly seen. Average normal visibility distance is 16km or 10 Miles.

Impaired visbility is when there is an obstruction of visbility which causes the distance of an object or light are indistincly seen. According to the international definition impaired visbility can be cause by fog, haze, mist, freezing drizzle, heavy rain and etc.

  • Fog: less than 1km
  • Haze: Between 2km and 5km
  • Mist: Between 1km and 2km
  • Freezing Drizzle: under 900 Meters
  • Rain: less than 1km
